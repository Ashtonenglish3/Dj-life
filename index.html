About

Ashton English also known as Tremor is an upcoming psytrance dj.He specializes in bouncy prog that makes the crowd skit and never want to leave the dance floor.His journey began not too long ago when he played his first gig - Wardens Playground.Shortly after that he played at Summergrounds and made the crowd lose their minds and that's what made his passion grow to please the crowd.

People like me, DJs and producers, have a bigger say and a bigger voice than we've ever had before.
True inspiration overrides all fears. When you are inspired, you enter a trance state and can accomplish things that you may never have felt capable of doing.
My passion grows when i see people listens to my music and connects to it.
